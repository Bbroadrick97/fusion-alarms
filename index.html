Fully-Integrated Security Protection for Your Business

Fusion Alarms is a licensed and insured low voltage integrator serving Middle TN and surrounding areas. We specialize in video surveillance, access control, fire alarm/voice evac, intrusion detection, and intercom.

Modern Solutions to all your Security Needs

We are proud to provide services ranging from small, stand-alone devices to large, fully integrated systems and everything in between.

At Fusion Alarms, we believe a quality system is essential to keeping your business monitored and secure.

We work alongside you to make custom systems that fit your specific needs. Our goal is to be with you every step of the way, and to answer any questions you might have.
