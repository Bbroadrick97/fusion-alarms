Our Solutions

At Fusion Alarms, we understand that low-voltage systems can be complex and sometimes overwhelming. We operate on a simple philosophy - design the system as if we owned the building.

This means that when we are designing your system, we consider the best protection possible, without trying to sell you anything that you don't need. What drives us is doing it one time, the right way.

Video Surveillance

A video surveillance system is one of the best ways to protect your business, employees, clients, and assets. When you work with us, we will be there every step of the way and treat your business as if we were designing and installing it at our own facility.

Access Control

At Fusion Alarms, we take pride in offering access control systems that provide security and functionality to your business.

Intrustion Detection

Intrusion Detection is an important aspect to protecting your business and everything in it. We utilize a combination of wired and/or wireless devices from industry leading manufacturers to bring together a robust, yet simple to use, intrusion system that's sure to help you sleep at night.

Fire and Life Safety

We offer systems to protect your business against fire and special hazards. Our team works alongside local Fire Marshalls and abides by codes in order to give your business the protection it needs.

Paging and Intercom
